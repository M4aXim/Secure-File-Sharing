Reporting Abuse

If you believe that someone is abusing FileShare to launch attacks, distribute malicious files, or violate our Terms of Service, please send us an email with all relevant evidence.

If you are the victim of a cyberattack or harassment and believe our service is involved, we encourage you to:

  • Report the incident to your local police or national cybercrime agency.
  • Email us directly at IT@maksimmalbasa.in.rs with any logs, links, or screenshots that help us identify the abuse.

We take abuse seriously and will fully cooperate with law enforcement investigations as required.
